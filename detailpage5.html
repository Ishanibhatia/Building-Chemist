Steel Protector

  • It is designed to protect for Steel, Timber, Concrete, Fibre Glass to shutters and easy moulds.
  • Corrosion resistance: Steel protectors form a barrier on the surface of steel, protecting it from exposure to moisture, oxygen, and other corrosive elements. This helps prevent rust and corrosion, extending the lifespan of the steel structure.
  • Heat resistance: Steel protectors may offer resistance to high temperatures, which is important for steel structures exposed to heat or fire hazards. These coatings can provide a protective barrier, delaying the transfer of heat to the steel substrate.
  • Chemical resistance: Some steel protectors are designed to provide resistance against chemical substances, such as acids, alkalis, solvents, or pollutants. This protects the steel surface from chemical damage and degradation.
