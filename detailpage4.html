LW

  • Building Chemist's LW is an Integral Waterproofing Compound for Plaster & Concrete
  • It is an integral liquid waterproofing admixture for concrete, and cement mortar used in basements roof slabs and screeds water retaining structures, external plastering, bathrooms and balconies, water, tank, sumps and drains, etc
  • METHOD OF APPLICATION: Add 200 ml of BUILDING CHEMIST LW for every 50 kg cement in the concrete/ morter mix The recommended dose of BUILDING CHEMIST'LW is mixed into the gauging water required to prepare concrete / moter. The concrete or morter should be mixed for 2-3
  • PRECAUTIONS & LIMITATIONS: Do not add BUILDING CHEMIST'LW directly to cement and maintain water Cement ratio as low as possible.
