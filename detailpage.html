Wood Protector

  • Wood Protector is a unique solution specifically designed for wood, offering three essential benefits: preservation, prevention, and curing. It is important to handle Wood Protector with care by avoiding direct contact with bare hands and inhalation. We recommend the use of hand gloves and head cover for safety.
  • Our Pine Oil Base Solutions are ideal for achieving superior results when using Wood Protector before painting, polishing, or laminating. For effective application, dip the wooden particle into the solution or apply it through coating or spraying, as desired.
  • Wood Protector is suitable for protecting all types of wood and can also help address the issue of powder falling in plywood by injecting it into the pinhole.
  • To use Wood Protector, start by cleaning the surface, and then apply it using a brush, spray gun, or swab. If necessary, a second coat can be applied. Allow 8 hours of drying time before proceeding with lamination, painting, or polishing.
  • It is important to note that Wood Protector is a ready-to-use solution and should not be mixed with any additional compositions or oils.
  • Please be aware that the solvents used in this formulation are not regulated under the Insecticide Act of 1968. Therefore, no trading license is required for the use and distribution of Wood Protector.
